Reward System Walkthrough

Daily Bonus System

Track your daily rewards and increase your earnings with consistent logins. One claim per day with growing bonuses.

Scratch & Win

Scratch cards daily to reveal coin rewards. Limited scratches available each day to maintain fairness and excitement.

Watch & Earn

Watch short advertisements to earn bonus coins. Restrictions on number of ads ensure balance in the system.

Spin & Win

Try your luck with the spin wheel. Each spin grants random coin amounts with cool 3D visual effects on the wheel.

Promo Codes

Use promo codes to gain additional rewards. Share codes with friends or find them in special campaigns.

Captcha Fill & Earn

Earn coins by solving captchas. This feature helps maintain user engagement and prevents automated abuse.

Playground Interface
